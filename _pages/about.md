<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

I am a PhD student in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at <a href="https://www.psu.edu/" target="_blank">The Pennsylvania State University</a>, where I am co-advised by Professor <a href="https://suhangwang.ist.psu.edu/" target="_blank">Suhang Wang</a> and Professor <a href="https://faculty.ist.psu.edu/xzz89/" target="_blank">Xiang Zhang</a>. I was an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Web Services (AWS) in California. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from <a href="https://english.cqu.edu.cn" target="_blank">Chongqing University</a>. My recent research interests lie in multimodal learning, language models, and graph learning.
